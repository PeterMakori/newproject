E-Noticeboard system facilitates communication from the faculty representative (Dean) and the departmental representative (COD) to the students.
The user's accounts are created by the system administrator. Your Registration number is your password by default. Students must ensure they change their password after their firts log in.
Students will log in using their registration numbers. The registration number will be the default password which you will be required to use when Loging in for the first time. On your first login, ensure you change the password using the Profile link provided.
The notices are organized and vsible in respect to your faculy and department. The notices are displayed immediately they are created and will not be visble whtn thie expiry date arrives. Once they login, the student will get an alert showing if a new notice has been posted from the faculty or department. If there is a new notice, then they click the button to redirect then to view and read the new notice. But if there is no any new notice that has been posted, they can click the sidebar ‘Notices’ link and then navigate to the previous read notices by either clicking ‘faculty’ to view read faculty notices or ‘department’ to view department read notices.
You can Search notices within a specifi range period using the dates in the search field. The students also can search notices by due date which is the date on which the posted notices will expire and no longer be displayed on the notice. This date is specified by the creator of the notice depending on the date which they deem the notice to be unnecessary for the display.
You will be required to update your email and change your password only in the profile menu. The ‘profile' link on the sidebar which will allow them to view their profile. You will also edit your email address only using the edit tab under profile. Moreover, the students can also change or update their password using the ‘change password’ link.
